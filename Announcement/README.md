```
AnnouncementBar
├── Marquee Track (scrolling text)
│   ├── "Extra discounts of ₹650 at checkout"
│   ├── "Hurry Up, Shop Now!"
│   ├── "50% Off"
│   ├── Countdown Timer (HH:MM:SS)
│   └── "Save Min 50% on all orders and get free shipping"
└── Diamond separators (◆)
```
